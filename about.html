About Us

It all began during the COVID-19 pandemic when Cayla discovered the world of crocheting and Amigurumi. Not long after learning how to make her first tiny stress ball, she was "hooked" (yes, pun-intended).

She then began making key chains, Christmas ornaments, and stuffed animals for friends and family. After the encouragement from love ones around her, she decided to start this usiness and share her love of crochet and cute thigs to the rest of the world.

CiciCrochet sells handmade croceted accessories and toys on Etsy.
